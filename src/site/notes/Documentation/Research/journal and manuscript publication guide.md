### email draft
```markdown
**Subject:** Manuscript Submission – **manuscript title**

Dear Editor-in-Chief,

Please find attached our manuscript titled **“manuscript title”** for consideration as a **manuscript type** in _publication company name_.

The manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under consideration by any other journal. The required cover letter and author’s declaration form are also attached.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,  
[Corresponding Author’s Full Name]  
[Affiliation]  
[Email Address]
```
### whitelisting email to prevent feedback getting to spam folder
- add publication company email to contacts
- add filters
- if email enters spam folder remove remove manually and add to allow list
- quickly search for email using `from:editor@wjaets.com`
